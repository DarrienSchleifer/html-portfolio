The Bachelor of Arts in Digital Media allows students to integrate the multiple domains of art, storytelling, and technology. It is designed to provide a solid foundation in techniques and theory in one focused area of competence, provide a broad understanding of related disciplines including arts, humanities and technology, and provide extended experience in working in multidisciplinary teams on realistic problems. In the Web and Social Platforms track, students combine the artistic skills of graphic designers and technical skills of programmers to create compelling, innovative solutions.
